HOME DESIGN TARGET AUDIENCE

Target Audience

The target audience of the San Tan Valley Chamber of Commerce are the local business owners and the local community.

Personas

Persona 1: Joseph Kimball

Joseph Kimball is 40 year old member of the community. He is a rodeo cowboy with a passion for anything western. He recently opened a tack and feed store in the San Tan Valley area. He loves supporting other small businesses and is looking to use this website to collaborate with other business owners in the area.

Persona 2: Rachel Freeman

Rachel is a life long resident of San Tan Valley. She loves the outdoors, loves to try new things and meet new people. She is a young mother of two and is looking to use this website to find local events in the area that are geared towards families.

Scenarios

  • Newlywed couple new to the area, looking for local businesses to patronize.
  • Investor looking to start a business in the area.
  • Looking for resources for making local connections.
  • Long time resident looking for local events to attend.
  • Current business owner looking to expand within the community.
  • Tourists looking for holiday events in the area.
