Target Audience

The target audience of the San Tan Valley Chamber of Commerce are the local business owners and the local community.

Personas

  • Persona 1: Joseph Kimball

    • Local small business owner who became a recent member of the San Tan Valley Chamber of Commerce. He is hoping to connect with the local community and promote his small business.

  • Persona 2: Rachel Freeman

    • Long time resident of San Tan Valley who is heavily involved in the community and helps to promote local businesses.

Scenarios

1. Newlywed couple new to the area, looking for local businesses to patronize

2. Investor looking to start a business in the area. Looking for resources for making local connections.

3. Long time resident looking for local events to attend.

4. Current business owner looking to expand within the community.

5. Tourists looking for holiday events in the area.
