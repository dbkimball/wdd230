Purpose

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to provide a platform for our business that specializes in organic, locally grown foods. This online presence will reflect the company's mission to provide healthy and fresh food options while promoting convenience, customatization and community engagement.

  • Hero Section with appealing image
  • Business Contact Info
  • Summary about coastal area with text and images
  • Call to action button for users to create own drink
  • Social media links
  • Weather Card
  • Info Card

Target Audience

The target audience for Bountiful Foods includes individuals who prioritize fresh, healthy and organic food options, with a specific focus on the local commnunity and tourists from the coastal areas of Southern California. People who seek convenience and customization in their food choices but are also health conscious. They value sustainable and ethical practices in food production and are interested in supporting small businesses. The target audience may consist of families, tourists as well as the local food connoisseur.
