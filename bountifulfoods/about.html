About Bountiful Foods

We specialize in organic, locally grown foods. Our purpose is to provide healthy and fresh food options while promoting convenience, customatization and community engagement.

Bountiful Foods has been serving the Carlsbad area since 2012. The majority of our produce is obtained directly from farmers in the area. Our main location is located in Carlsbad but we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We pride ourself in providing fresh and healthy food options. Not only do we offer made to order drinks but fruit bags as well. Come visit us today!
