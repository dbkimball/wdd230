San Tan Valley Arizona

Established: 2003

Population: 104,936

San Tan Valley is located in the southeastern part of Maricopa County. It was established when the area borading it to the east merged with Queen Creek. The name was chosen due to its location along the San Tan Mountains.

San Tan Campus - Central AZ College

3736 E. Bella Vista Road, San Tan Valley, AZ 85143

The San Tan Campus is the newest Central Arizona College location. This beautiful facility features more than 72,000 square feet of space and offers an array of academic degrees and certificates. It's a full service campus featuring a student center, library and state of the art classrooms and labs.

San Tan Mountains at Sunset

Arizona is known as "The Valley of the Sun". Dry conditions and upper-level clous create an ideal mix for those beautiful pink, orange and purple hues. The San Tan Mountains rank in the top 10 places to view an Arizona Sunset.

San Tan Mountain Regional Park

Sunday-Thursday: 6am to 8pm

Friday-Saturday 6am to 10pm

Open 365 days a year!

San Tan Mountain Regional Park is a large rural/suburban park located in San Tan Valley. It encompasses over 10,000 acres of Sonoran Desert Beauty. Elevation ranges from about 1400 feet to over 2500 feet. Various types of wildlife may be seen, including reptiles, birds and mammals.
